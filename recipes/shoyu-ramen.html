Shoyu Ramen

About the Dish

Shoyu, soy sauce, ramen is a savory noodle soup dish. It's delicious, what more can I say? :)

The recipe source: pickled plum.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp of sesame oil
  • 1 clove of minced garlic
  • 1 1-inch piece of peeled and minced ginger
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of sake
  • 1 cup of dashi
  • 2 1/2 tb of mirin
  • 1 cup of ramen broth (the broth the ramen noodles are cooking in)
  • 1 soft hard-boiled egg (then slice into 2)
  • menma (pickled bamboo)
  • nori sheet
  • chopped scallions
  • ground white pepper

Instructions

  1. Place a small pot over medium-high heat. Add sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© for 2 minutes.
  2. Add soy sauce, sake, dashi, and mirin to the pot. Bring to a boil. Then, lower the heat, cover, and simmer on low for 15 minutes. Set aside.
  3. Place a pot of water on medium heat. Bring to a boil.
  4. Add ramen noodles and cook according to directions on package.
  5. Before draining the noodles, scoop 1 cup of the ramen noodle broth and add it to the small pot (shoyu broth). Stir.
  6. Drain the noodles and divide them into 2 bowls.
  7. Pour soup into bowls and add toppings.
